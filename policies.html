Jeté Dance Center

Studio Policies

Jeté Dance Center rules and policies are for the enjoyment and safety of all students.

Please carefully read and follow.

There are specific risks of physical or property damages, losses, or injury that may result from your child's participation with Jeté Dance Center. You voluntarily assume the risks associated with such participation and agree not to hold Jeté Dance Center responsible for any damages or injuries to your child or property. Our policies need to be read and agreed to prior to participation in class. Payment must be up to date before attending class.

Registration

Registration requires the completion of an information form, payment of a registration fee, first month’s tuition, and your signature (or electronic signature) on our policy agreement. You are acknowledging that you understand our monthly tuition charges and the procedure for discontinuing classes. The registration fee is non-refundable. It insures you a place in class, assures us that you intend to be there, and covers the business clerical costs associated with your enrollment at Jeté Dance Center.

Class Etiquette

No chewing gum allowed in class.

No food is allowed in the studio room, only water.

No cell phone use in class.

Cell phones must be turned off or on silent.

Be attentive and positive.

Foul or disruptive language will NOT be tolerated.

Respect your classmates.

Wear cover ups to and from class.

No talking in class.

No running in studio.

Dancers are not allowed to enter studio without teacher present.

Always thank your teacher before exiting the studio.

Dancers who do not follow studio rules or are disruptive to other students will be asked to leave class and return the following week.

Placement

Students will be placed in classes according to age and ability level. The instructor reserves the right to move a student to another class based on what is best for that dancer, their maturity, and work ethic in class.

Unfortunately, we cannot place dancers in classes to accommodate family schedules or carpools.

Attendance

Students are asked to arrive on time. Students more than 15 minutes late will not be allowed in class as it is disruptive to your fellow dancers. You may be asked to sit and watch the remainder of the class.

Absences and tardies have adverse effects:

Students who are late or absent miss valuable training/learning time.

Excessive class absences may result in not performing in the Summer Showcase.

Make Up Classes

A limit of one make-up class is offered per month and you must schedule the make-up class yourself in the parent portal.

A makeup class can be taken in the same month that a class was missed. To arrange a makeup class, please notify the instructor when you arrive that you are making up a class.

If a student is sick (including any fevers), please do not attend class. We do not want the dancer to get worse or transmit the illness to their classmates. If the student is not contagious and would still like to come and observe class, please email the studio.

Arrival/Departure

Parents and/or guardians must come into the studio to pick up the students younger than 7 years of age. We appreciate parents arriving a few minutes before class ends in order to wait for their dancer to come out of class. No student should leave the studio without a parent or guardian. We are very sensitive to students’ whereabouts and their safety; however, Jeté Dance Center will not be held responsible for your child.

Any student left unattended at the studio for longer than 30 minutes before or after the class they have registered for will be charged as if they attended an additional dance class. The same holds true for siblings. Do not leave siblings unattended at the studio.

Lobby Etiquette

Parents and family are not allowed to sit inside the classroom at any time, but welcome to view classes on a weekly basis from the viewing window in the lobby. Please refrain from interrupting class and do no walk into the classroom during class time. Videotaping of classes will not be permitted, unless the instructor says otherwise.

Communication

Instructors are available to speak with you by appointment only.

We know how important it is to work together to make sure that each student is progressing along in skills and confidence, but conferencing in between classes or at the end of the evening is not effective and distracts the teacher from starting the next class. Please email your instructor to schedule an appointment. Instructors will respond within 24 hours.

Tuition

JDC enrollment is billed based on the number of dance hours per week. Monthly payments will be made in equal installments. Tuition is due on the 1st of the month and late on the 6th.

Classes with less than three students are subject to cancellation.

Tuition is non-refundable and non-transferable for missed classes including holidays or vacations. JDC requires a 30-day written notice to drop out of classes.

Auto-Pay payments: Monthly tuition payments will be charged the first day of each month to the credit card on file. A late fee of $15 will be added on the 6th each month. Late fees will not be taken off of accounts and are non-refundable.

A non-refundable, annual tuition fee of $30 will be charged to ensure your place in class and cover business clerical costs associated to your enrollment at JDC.

Financial Obligation

If there is a financial concern, please contact the directors.

Jeté Dance Center's Showcases

JDC has annual productions showcasing studio class routines each season. Dates are not announced until confirmed.

Recital fees include:

Non-refundable Costume fee per class performing in showcase.

Non-refundable Production fee.

Showcase tickets (Available for purchase after dates are announced).

Withdrawal/Cancellation

A 30-day written notice to JDC is required, you may send email to jetedancetx@gmail.com.

Fees for the current month will still be due in full.

Registration fees and Tuition fees are non-refundable.

A $25 processing fee may be added to students that withdraw from the studio.

Photography

Jeté Dance Center has my permission to photograph my child in class or at recitals. I grant Jeté Dance Center permission to use photographs of my child on the JDC website design or marketing tools.

If I am not comfortable with my child being photographed in the studio, I will contact the office in writing.

Studio Closures

The studio will be closed for all major holidays. Exact dates of closure will be noted on the studio calendar. Regarding closures due to bad weather, please check the studio social media and/or email before you leave, as we will be updating you about class cancellations.
