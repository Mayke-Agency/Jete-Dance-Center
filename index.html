About Our Studio

Welcome, we are Courtney and Nicole, the owners and founders of Jeté Dance Center. JDC was created in 2024 by two best friends turned sisters-in-law. We uphold family values and believe being moms has helped shape us into better educators. Both coming from studio backgrounds, we have trained, performed, and competed most of our lives. We created a safe space to nurture the art we love so much. As teachers, we offer structured and personal education.

We train our dancers in classical technique and create performers that exude passion and confidence.

Dance Classes for All Ages

Jeté Dance Center offers a safe and fun learning environment that fosters confidence and self-esteem. Whether you are looking to dance as a recreation or would like to pursue a professional career, we can assist you in achieving your goals. Our discipline, creativity, and passion will inspire dancers of all ages.

Each class is designed and personalized to the dancers that are in them. Every student is unique, and our goal is to help you become your best. We pride ourselves on a controlled class setting where each dancer is a priority. JDC believes the lessons you learn in the studio will benefit character development and life skills.
